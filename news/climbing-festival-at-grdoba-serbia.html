Zlatna Lopta sports club, together with Mountaineering association of Serbia (PSS) and KES Armadilo, are proud to host a climbing event in one of the post popular climbing area in Serbia, Grdoba Valjevo, on 5th and 6th of October, 2019.

This Festival will bring together climbers from different climbing disciplines to test out 15 new routes, freshly bolted for this occasion and to climb over 80 old classics. Besides climbing, participants will have a chance to experience other outdoor activities too. If you are a fan of hiking, yoga, meditation or zip line, save the date and visit Grdoba, Valjevo.

Whether you are part of a club, an individual, experienced climber or a rookie, take your gear and come join the climbing festival at Grdoba! Entry is FREE!

Additional info

  • Parking is limited. Please consider sharing lifts to get to the crag.
  • Respect other people and allow space for others to pass.
  • Camping is possible, but also other types of accommodation.
  • Local supermarket is nearby.
  • Keep the environment clean.
